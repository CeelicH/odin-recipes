Carbonara Recipe


Description

This is a fast and easy carbonara that can be made with simple ingredients and under 10 minutes

Only a simple pot and cooking pan are needed for this recipe


Ingredients needed

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Mushrooms
  • Sliced ham
  • Pepper
  • Leaf
  • Mushroom gravy

Steps to make recipe

  1. Cook noodles
  2. Make sauce and add toppings
  3. Strain noodles
  4. Prep toppings
  5. Add gravy and toppings
